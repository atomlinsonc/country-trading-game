🌐 Country Trading

Lead your nation to economic prosperity through smart production choices and strategic trade.

How to Play

  • Choose your country's specialization – this is your comparative advantage.
  • Each round, set your production sliders to decide how many units of each good you make.
  • You have limited production capacity – allocate wisely.
  • Sell goods on the world market. Prices rise when supply is scarce.
  • Other countries will shift their production over time. Watch the market – if a country stops making a good, its price soars and you'll pay more to import it.
  • The game lasts 20 rounds. After each round, review the results then click End Turn to advance.
  • Maximize your treasury by the end of Round 20!

Choose Your Specialization
